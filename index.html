Welcome to Agromitra

Agromitra is a product developed by D'RAGAS. It is an innovative solution designed to assist farmers in protecting their crops from animal damage. Agromitra utilizes advanced technology, including ultrasonic sensors, to detect the presence of animals and deter them from approaching the crops.

With its unique features and capabilities, Agromitra aims to provide farmers with a reliable, cost-effective, and eco-friendly solution to mitigate crop damage caused by animals, thereby promoting more sustainable and efficient farming practices.
